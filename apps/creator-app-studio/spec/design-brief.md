# Design Brief

Target app: `apps/creator-app-studio`

## Design Objective

Create a premium, calm, modern landing page that feels closer to a product studio or consumer-tech brand than a generic SaaS homepage.

## Visual Direction

- Warm ivory background: `#F7F3EC`
- Near-black text: `#171411`
- Soft charcoal supporting text: `#5F5851`
- Warm surface cards: `#FFFDF8`
- Stone borders: `#DED6CB`
- Accent violet: `#5B4DFF`
- Accent dark: `#2E246E`

## UX Priorities

- Strong visual hierarchy and spacing rhythm
- Editorial typography
- Sticky or simple anchor navigation
- Responsive hero that stacks cleanly
- Premium form card with polished success and error messaging
- Minimal motion only where it helps the experience feel refined

## Avoid

- Generic SaaS hero patterns
- Loud gradients or flashy startup visuals
- Cluttered dashboards
- Aggressive sales language
