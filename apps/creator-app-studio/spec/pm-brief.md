# PM Brief

Target app: `apps/creator-app-studio`

## Product Objective

Build a premium landing website for a service that helps SMB creators launch branded web apps or website experiences their audiences can pay for.

## Target Customer

SMB influencers and creators with a meaningful niche audience and trusted personal brand, but without an internal product, engineering, or growth team.

## Core Promise

We turn audience trust into a polished digital product designed around the creator's brand, built for their fans, and monetized from day one.

## Primary Conversion

Submit a short lead form or request a consult.

## Secondary Actions

- Browse example app concepts
- Understand how the process works
- Understand the build fee plus revenue share model at a high level
- Share a rough product idea

## Non-goals

- No client dashboard in v1
- No marketplace
- No payment collection implementation
- No niche-specific overfitting
- No generic SaaS positioning
